BLooDY GooD PERIoD

is a charity that provides feminine hygeine products to women in poverty, refugees, or without access to the products.

The comission was to simply make something big, unique, and not too much pink...

I wanted to highlight on the importance of these products, and how life changing they are; hence the the 'Tarot Card' style and symbols associated with life, death & fate.

It was important to include the gender fluid symbol to represent the charity's support for the trans community.
