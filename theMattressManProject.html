THE MATTRESS MAN

is a company that disposes of old mattresses so that they can be recycled properly. They do household collection so wanted to get the word out that you can call them rather than taking it to a tip.

The brief was very open and the client just wanted an illustration of a man carrying a mattress.

Inspired by the community aspect of it, I wanted to create a retro looking 'friendly/helpful neighbour' look to the man.

The striped branding emulated the lines and patterns on a mattress and the green because of the eco aspect of the company.
