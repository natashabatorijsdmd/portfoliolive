PIGZINE

is a magazine that follows an art project, Pig by Kaleider, around Europe.

The point of Pigzine is to document what happens when Pig is placed in a public space, so that the stories can be compared and shared.

The brief was very loose and almost all creative direction was handed to me, so I planned each individual widget on the website individually and then arranged them on the page in a magazine-esque layout.

using design elementf from the Pig itself, the website background and title shows off the bolted transparent casing anf the red LED light. This design is also visible on the red stickers which were printed on a large scale to distribute around the european locations.
