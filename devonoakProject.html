DEVoNoAK

is a family run company that creates traditional Timber framed buildings using a mixture of old techniques and modern technology.

The brief was to create something simple, creative and friendly, because a modern site with lots of white-space would not fit their brand nor would it show off their crafstmanship and connection to the traditional techniques.

The company didn't have a logo yet, but in a photo I had seen that their trucks had been painted with striking blues and yellows. This work happened to be by a local craftsman who createshand painted traditional signs - so I used the truck's design to create a digital logo.

The website and business cards include the hand drawn oak leaf illustrations to add another element to the design which communicates craftsmanship, appreciation for oak, and a non-corporate personality. They fit with the employees' love and knowledge for trees, and offer something else for visitors to the site to look at.
