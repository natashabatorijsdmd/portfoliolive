Nature & Therapy UK

Website for national training courses in Shinrin-Yoku a.k.a 'Forest Bathing'. The main purpose of this site is for information on the practise of Shinrin-Yoku and booking onto the course. The site recieves a large amount of traffic so needs to be easy to find the booking page despite the large amounts of text and information. This is created to communicate the clean white style of japanese art, as well as the beautiful british forests.

Pigzine

Developed in collaboration with WebWise Media. Online magazine for an art project touring Europe.
