Archetyping is a blog exploring my interests, questions, & realisations about technology.
It includes my creative (and often experimental) projects as well as my writing and research.

I have always had a fascination with the the way modern technology can be used to explore and change the world. Technology shapes everything in society in one way or another, and yet there is an exponential amount that we don't understand about it's potential or effects. There is actually so much experimenting, inventing, and tinkering to be done; that the role of the tech-pioneer is being subverted away from the huge companies (who own the means and money), to anyone with an idea

My interest in technology, much like the content of these posts, is not solely about technology. It is about what we can do with it. I am currently in a process of exploring how
it is changing my world, and how I can change the world around me...

...and thats what I'm here to talk about.
