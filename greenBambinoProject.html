GREEN BAMBINo

is a Nutritional Therapist specialising in pre-natal and post-natal health, children's health, & fertility.

The brief was to create something for a social media presence that was clean, airy, and fresh. For the name, the client had a list and we managed to workshop it down into one final name - whilst creating the painting-style logo of a vegetable that the client had imagined.

We tried the logo with a few different vegetables before deciding on the pea - It has an implication of children and babies, especially when in a pod!

The client wanted a colour palette that was clean, light, and fresh - with muted tones of green. The colours were provided as social media tiles to be written on for recipes and quotes. The 'highlight covers' with hand-drawn style symbols also carry the fresh and clean brand personality.
